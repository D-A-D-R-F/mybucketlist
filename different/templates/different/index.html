Welcome to #mybucketlist

A simple application to help you fullfill those things you have always wanted to do , but never ended up doing!


Features

1. My Bucket - Add to the endless list of things you have always wanted to do, but never did!

2. Random Activities - Complete an extremely random activity to get rid of your boredom!

3. My Schedule - A random task from your bucket list for you to complete!

4. Connect - A chance to interact with people in your area and make time for friends!

5. Share - Share completed items with other users and read from experiences of other users.

6. Daily Quote - An inspirational quote to boost your motivation each day!
